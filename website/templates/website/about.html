About Django Namibia

The official community for Django developers in Namibia, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Our Story

How It All Began

Django Namibia was founded in 2016 by a group of passionate developers under the umbrella of the Python Software Association of Namibia (PyNAM). We recognized the need for a dedicated Django community in Namibia.

As an official working group of PyNAM, what started as informal meetups in Windhoek has grown into a national community with members across the country. Our first official event brought together 30 developers, and since then we've been organizing workshops, hackathons, and networking events to grow the Django ecosystem in Namibia.

Our PyNAM Affiliation

Django Namibia is an initiative of the Python Software Association of Namibia (PyNAM), established to serve as the country's official Django community. PYNAM is the official body representing Python developers in Namibia. This affiliation connects us with the broader Python community while allowing us to focus specifically on Django development.

Our Growth

With the support of PyNAM, we've grown to over 200+ members across Namibia. Our community includes beginners learning web development, experienced Django developers, and companies building products with Django.

Through our PyNAM affiliation, we've partnered with local tech companies, educational institutions, and the global Django community to bring world-class resources and opportunities to Namibian developers.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our community and shape everything we do

Practical Learning

  • Project-based workshops

Strategic Partnerships

  • Industry collaborations
  • Education partnerships

Our Mission

"To develop Namibia's Django community by providing accessible education, creating professional opportunities, and fostering collaborations that benefit both developers and the nation's growing tech industry."
